What is Land Tracker?

Land Tracker is a free tool that enables Pixel players to track their preferred lands, choices, and view their status in real-time regarding the usage of windmills, furnaces, carpentry tables, grills, trees, and more. This tool is updated to prevent overload when receiving large numbers of user requests to view the lands, as it features a cooldown mechanism that maintains stability between requests and servers.
